Customer Recovery Console — Mac Install Instructions

1. Choose the correct DMG:
   - Apple Silicon Mac: arm64.dmg
   - Intel Mac: x64.dmg
2. Double-click the .dmg file.
3. Drag Customer Recovery Console into Applications.
4. Open it from Applications.
5. Import only files you are authorized to use.

Note: Early/internal builds may show a macOS security warning until the app is signed and notarized.
